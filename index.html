WHO we are?

Experts General Contracting is a newly setup company takes from Bury of Greater Manchester as its base location and aims to operate within the Greater Manchester Area with plans to expand all over the United Kingdom.

The Company is formed by tow managing partners whom has decided to join hand in hand putting their years of experience to accumulate each other for the benefit of business and targeted clients.

The company had set up its code of conduct standard and will assure its enforcement among all its projects, our code is (Standards Applied) this means that company operations shall assure the state of the art specifications, regulations and British Standards of construction are followed and applied in all aspects of our projects life cycle.
